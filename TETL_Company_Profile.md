# Two Elephants Technologies LLP (TETL) - Company Profile

## Overview
- **Full Name**: Two Elephants Technologies LLP (TETL)
- **Legacy**: 65 years of industrial trust, starting in 1960 as Pushpa Textile in Solapur.
- **Slogan**: "From Looms to Labs" — bridging industrial precision with digital execution.
- **Mission**: To create opportunity in Solapur for local talent to work on global problems.
- **Vision**: To become a global leader in high-stakes enterprise technology while staying rooted in Solapur.
- **Headquarters**: Solapur, Maharashtra, India.
- **Global Presence**: India, USA (Houston, Texas), Middle East.
- **Values**: Strength, Care, Honesty, Domain Depth, Long-term Thinking, Guided Growth.
- **Symbolism**: Mother Elephant (Wisdom/Legacy) + Baby Elephant (Growth/Digital Future).

## Services
1. **Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)**: Tailored for textile, pharma, BFSI and SME sectors.
2. **Cybersecurity & Compliance**: VAPT, RBI/DPDP/FDA compliance frameworks, SOC operations.
3. **FinTech & Banking Solutions**: Core banking, digital payments, and NBFC platforms.
4. **AI / ML & Intelligent Automation**: Predictive analytics, RPA workflows, cognitive AI.
5. **Building Management System (BMS)**: HVAC and energy optimization (20-30% energy reduction).
6. **Facility Management System (FMS)**: Digital work orders, maintenance scheduling.
7. **Fault Detection & Diagnostics (FDD)**: AI-based anomaly detection (70% downtime reduction).
