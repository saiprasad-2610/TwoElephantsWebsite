OUR STORY

A Legacy That Earns Its Next Chapter

Every great technology company is built on something real. Ours is built on 65 years of it.

The Rathi family — known in Solapur as "Do Haathi," the Two Elephants — built Pushpa Textiles into a company that supplies IndiGo, Raymond, and Maruti Suzuki without compromise. Then they built an Oil & Gas logistics company from Houston, Texas, serving America's most demanding industrial sector.

They never entered an industry they didn't understand deeply. Two Elephants Technologies is no different.
